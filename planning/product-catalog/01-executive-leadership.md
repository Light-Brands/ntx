## EX-01: AI Readiness Assessment

**Price:** $2,500 - $4,000
**Tier:** Discovery
**Delivery:** Comprehensive assessment report with scoring and recommendations

### Description

A thorough evaluation of an organization's current state readiness for AI adoption, covering technology infrastructure, data maturity, organizational culture, talent capabilities, and strategic alignment.

### Deliverables

- Executive Summary (2-3 pages)
- Readiness Scorecard across 6 dimensions
- Gap Analysis with prioritized recommendations
- Quick Wins identification (30-60-90 day)
- Risk Assessment overview

### Generation Prompt

```
You are a senior AI strategy consultant preparing an AI Readiness Assessment for [COMPANY_NAME], a [INDUSTRY] company with [EMPLOYEE_COUNT] employees and approximately $[REVENUE] in annual revenue.

Create a comprehensive AI Readiness Assessment Report with the following structure:

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Provide a 2-page executive summary covering:
- Overall readiness score (1-100) with brief justification
- Top 3 strengths positioning the organization for AI success
- Top 3 critical gaps requiring immediate attention
- Strategic recommendation on AI adoption approach (conservative, moderate, aggressive)
- Expected ROI timeline for AI investments

## READINESS SCORECARD

Score each dimension 1-10 with detailed justification:

### 1. Data Foundation (Weight: 25%)
Evaluate:
- Data quality and consistency across systems
- Data governance policies and practices
- Data accessibility and integration capabilities
- Historical data availability for model training
- Real-time data pipeline readiness

### 2. Technology Infrastructure (Weight: 20%)
Evaluate:
- Cloud infrastructure maturity
- API ecosystem and integration capabilities
- Computing resources (GPU/TPU access)
- Security and compliance infrastructure
- Development and deployment tooling

### 3. Organizational Culture (Weight: 20%)
Evaluate:
- Leadership commitment to AI transformation
- Innovation mindset across teams
- Change management capabilities
- Cross-functional collaboration patterns
- Risk tolerance and experimentation culture

### 4. Talent & Skills (Weight: 15%)
Evaluate:
- Current AI/ML expertise in-house
- Data science and analytics capabilities
- Technical workforce adaptability
- Training and upskilling programs
- Ability to attract AI talent

### 5. Strategic Alignment (Weight: 10%)
Evaluate:
- Clarity of AI vision and objectives
- Alignment with business strategy
- Executive sponsorship strength
- Budget allocation for AI initiatives
- Success metrics definition

### 6. Use Case Pipeline (Weight: 10%)
Evaluate:
- Identified AI use cases and prioritization
- Business case development maturity
- Proof of concept track record
- Scaling capabilities for successful pilots
- Cross-departmental use case identification

## GAP ANALYSIS

For each dimension scoring below 7:
- Describe the specific gap
- Explain business impact of the gap
- Provide 3 actionable recommendations to close the gap
- Estimate resource requirements (time, budget, personnel)
- Define success metrics for gap closure

## QUICK WINS ROADMAP

Identify 6-9 quick wins across three timeframes:

### 30-Day Wins
- List 2-3 immediately achievable improvements
- Zero to minimal budget required
- High visibility, low risk

### 60-Day Wins
- List 2-3 short-term initiatives
- Moderate resource investment
- Foundation-building activities

### 90-Day Wins
- List 2-3 medium-term achievements
- Meaningful resource commitment
- Demonstrable business impact

## RISK ASSESSMENT

Identify and score (High/Medium/Low) risks across:
- Technical risks
- Organizational risks
- Talent risks
- Data/Privacy risks
- Competitive risks

For each High risk, provide mitigation strategy.

## RECOMMENDED NEXT STEPS

Provide prioritized list of 5-7 immediate actions with:
- Action description
- Owner role (not name)
- Timeline
- Dependencies
- Success criteria

Format the entire report professionally with clear headers, bullet points, and visual elements described in brackets where appropriate (e.g., [CHART: Radar chart showing 6-dimension scores]).
```
