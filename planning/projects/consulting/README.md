# Consulting Services | Project Specs

This folder contains client engagement specifications, templates, and active project documentation for the AI Brand Factory consulting arm.

---

## Core Concept

> **We do not build a "tool" first. We sell outcomes first. Then we build only what revenue validates.**

The AI Brand Factory consulting arm is the initial point of inbound for clients to experience our services. We transform inquiry into impact through three core offerings:

### 1. Soul Product: "We Give the Breath of Life to Your Story"
AI-assisted story excavation, narrative architecture, and book creation. This is our authority anchor—the product that positions the company and builds credibility.

### 2. Fastest Path to Revenue: AI Strategy Consulting
AI Business Acceleration Blueprints delivered in 5-7 days. We audit, map opportunities, recommend tools, and create 90-day roadmaps. This can be sold immediately.

### 3. High-Ticket Follow-On: AI Implementation
Building the AI systems: internal GPT agents, website AI, marketing automation, CRM workflows, and team training. This is where recurring revenue appears.
