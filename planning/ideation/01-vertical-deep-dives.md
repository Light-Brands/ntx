```

│  NETWORK: Creator Guilds, Collaboration Matching        │
│  COMMUNITY: Light Creators DAO                          │

```

### Key Opportunities

| Opportunity | Description | Moat |
|-------------|-------------|------|
| **AI-First Creation** | Tools that make AI feel like collaboration, not replacement | Intuition Engine integration |
| **Sovereign Distribution** | Own your audience, not platform dependency | Decentralized identity + direct relationships |
| **Creator Financing** | Advances against future earnings, tokenized | On-chain revenue history as collateral |
| **Collaborative IP** | Shared ownership of co-created works | Smart contract royalty splitting |

3. **Light Studio**
