# Consulting Services Agents

## 30-Day Revenue Blueprint | AI Brand Factory Consulting Arm

> **Core Principle**: We do not build a "tool" first. We sell outcomes first. Then we build only what revenue validates.

The Consulting Services agents orchestrate the AI Brand Factory's client-facing consulting arm—the initial point of inbound for clients to experience our services. This system transforms the 30-Day Revenue Blueprint into an executable, AI-powered delivery machine.

## The Unfair Advantage

**Story + Strategy + Speed + AI Leverage**

1. **AI Story & Book Creation** — The soul product (authority anchor)
2. **AI Strategy Consulting** — Fastest path to cash (blueprint delivery)
3. **AI Implementation** — High-ticket follow-on (recurring revenue)
