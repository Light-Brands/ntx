## Main Workflow

### Phase 1: Story Discovery (Sessions 1-3)

```
SESSION 1: The Archaeology of Self (90 min)
  PURPOSE: Unearth the raw material of the story

  EXPLORATION_AREAS:
    - Origin: Where did you begin?
    - Crucible: What forged you?
    - Awakening: When did everything change?
    - Mission: What are you here to do?
    - Wisdom: What have you learned?

  QUESTIONS:
    - "Tell me about a moment that changed everything."
    - "What did you believe before that you no longer believe?"
    - "What pain have you transmuted into purpose?"
    - "Who were you before the transformation?"
    - "What do you know now that you wish you knew then?"

  OUTPUT:
    - Raw story material (recorded/transcribed)
    - Key life events timeline
    - Emotional landscape map
```

```
SESSION 2: Theme & Truth (60 min)
  PURPOSE: Identify the core message and universal themes

  EXPLORATION_AREAS:
    - Central truth: What is THE thing you must say?
    - Universal themes: What makes this human?
    - Reader journey: What transformation do you offer?
    - Stakes: Why does this matter?

  QUESTIONS:
    - "If your reader takes away one thing, what is it?"
    - "What universal struggle does your story illuminate?"
    - "How will your reader be different after reading?"
    - "What's at stake if this story goes untold?"

  OUTPUT:
    - Core message statement
    - Theme matrix
    - Reader transformation arc
```

```
SESSION 3: Narrative Design (60 min)
  PURPOSE: Architect the structure and voice

  EXPLORATION_AREAS:
    - Structure: What's the shape of the story?
    - Voice: How do you want to sound?
    - Audience: Who is this for?
    - Format: Book? Memoir? Business book? Hybrid?

  QUESTIONS:
    - "Is this chronological or thematic?"
    - "Who is your ideal reader?"
    - "What other books are in conversation with this?"
    - "What's your natural way of speaking?"

  OUTPUT:
    - Narrative structure decision
    - Voice and tone guide
    - Audience profile
    - Format determination
```

### Phase 2: Narrative Architecture

```
DEVELOP book_architecture:

  STRUCTURE OPTIONS:
    - Chronological journey
    - Thematic exploration
    - Framework/methodology
    - Hero's journey
    - Hybrid approach

  CREATE chapter_framework:
    FOR each chapter:
      - Chapter title
      - Core story/content
      - Key message
      - Emotional beat
      - Transition to next

  DEVELOP writing_guidance:
    - Voice reminders
    - Do's and don'ts
    - Sample passages
    - Prompts for each section

  DELIVER narrative_architecture:
    - Complete outline document
    - Writing guide
    - Timeline and milestones
```

### Phase 3: Book Creation (Premium Only)

```
EXECUTE book_creation:

  FIRST_DRAFT:
    FOR each chapter:
      1. Review source material
      2. Generate AI-assisted draft
      3. Maintain voice consistency
      4. Include story elements
      5. Polish language
      6. Client review

  REVISION_ROUNDS:
    ROUND 1:
      - Structure refinement
      - Voice consistency
      - Story clarity
      - Message alignment

    ROUND 2:
      - Language polish
      - Flow optimization
      - Final details
      - Publication readiness

  FINAL_DELIVERY:
    - Complete manuscript
    - Publication guide
    - Marketing suggestions
```

## Story Excavation Framework

### The Archaeology Model

```
LAYER 1: Surface Story
  - What happened (events)
  - Who was involved
  - When and where

LAYER 2: Emotional Story
  - What you felt
  - What you feared
  - What you hoped

LAYER 3: Meaning Story
  - What you learned
  - How you changed
  - What became clear

LAYER 4: Universal Story
  - What this means for others
  - The timeless truth
  - The gift you offer
```
