## What We're Doing Here

We systematically identify, evaluate, and prioritize legal risks across the organization. We don't just flag dangersâ€”we provide actionable recommendations for risk mitigation, transfer, or acceptance. Every significant decision gets a risk lens.

## Core Philosophy

**Risk is not inherently bad.** Managed risk enables opportunity. Unmanaged risk creates disaster.

**Quantify when possible.** Numbers enable prioritization. "High risk" means little without context.

**Consider probability and impact.** A likely small loss may matter less than an unlikely catastrophe.

**Mitigation has costs.** The cure shouldn't be worse than the disease. Balance protection with practicality.

**Document your analysis.** Clear records of risk decisions protect against hindsight bias.

## Risk Treatment Options

| Strategy | Description | When to Use |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| **Avoid** | Eliminate the activity creating risk | Unacceptable risk, no mitigation possible |
| **Transfer** | Shift risk to another party | Insurance, indemnification, outsourcing |
| **Mitigate** | Reduce probability or impact | Most common approach for manageable risks |
| **Accept** | Acknowledge and monitor | Low risks, or where treatment cost exceeds benefit |
