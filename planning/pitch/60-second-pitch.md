# 60-Second Pitch

> For detailed introductions and pitch meetings

---

## The Pitch

Imagine it's 2026. AI can now build entire businesses — not just write code, but ideate products, create brands, deploy infrastructure, run marketing, and manage operations. All autonomously.

That's what we're building: the AI Brand Factory.

**How it works:** A network of specialized AI agents — each with a single clear job — working together in orchestrated pipelines. An Issue Manager creates the task, a Prep Agent sets up the environment, an Implementer writes the code, a Reviewer checks quality, and so on through validation and deployment.

**What makes it different:** Our Intuition Engine. Every agent learns from experience. They accumulate wisdom like humans do — recognizing patterns, applying past lessons, getting wiser with every action. This isn't just automation; it's artificial intuition.

**The infrastructure:** Every business we create is sovereign. Blockchain-native. DeFi-enabled. No central authority. No permission needed. Value flows freely across borders.

**The trajectory:** We're launching one business per week now. By 2027, we aim for daily launches. By 2030, unlimited scale — a factory running at the speed of thought.

**The commitment:** By end of 2026, we open-source everything. The entire platform, all the agents, the Intuition Engine, our path toward AGI. Zero restrictions. Anyone can fork it, evolve it, deploy their own instance.

**Why?** Because the goal isn't building a monopoly. It's building light-focused intelligence that exists to empower humanity — with tools for creation, healing, connection, and sovereign abundance.

We're building the path from today's AI to beneficial superintelligence. And we're making it a gift.

## Structure (60 seconds)

| Section | Duration | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Scene Setting | 10s | Paint the near-future picture |
| What We Build | 10s | The AI Brand Factory concept |
| How It Works | 15s | Agent architecture + Intuition Engine |
| Scale + Trajectory | 10s | Weekly → Daily → Unlimited |
| Open Source Commitment | 10s | The gift to humanity |
| Why / Vision | 5s | Light-focused superintelligence |
