## Presentation Notes

### Deck Design Guidelines

- Use VIBEUP visual identity (Deep Blue, Sky Blue, Off White)
- Minimal text per slide — let the message breathe
- Use imagery that reflects calm, connection, and nature
- Mira slide should include soft aqua glow elements
- Impact slide should show visual representation of trees/growth

### Speaking Points

**Problem (Slide 2):**  
"The paradox of our age: we're more connected than ever, yet more isolated. Social media has optimized for engagement, not wellbeing. Wellness apps have optimized for individual growth, not collective elevation. We believe there's a third way."

**Solution (Slide 4):**  
"VIBEUP is the first platform designed to elevate collective consciousness through aligned connection, intentional practice, and regenerative impact. It's social media meets wellness meets conscious discovery."

**Mira (Slide 6):**  
"What makes VIBEUP different is Mira. She's not a chatbot reading from a script. She's an emotionally intelligent presence that learns your energy, understands your intentions, and guides you with the care of a trusted friend."

**Vision (Slide 11):**  
"We're building infrastructure for a new way of being human together. Where technology elevates consciousness instead of fracturing attention. Where social platforms create real wellbeing, not just engagement metrics."

### Deck Variants

**Investor Deck:** Add financial projections, market analysis, team slide  
**Partner Deck:** Emphasize business opportunities, marketplace ecosystem  
**Press Deck:** Focus on cultural impact, user stories, mission-driven angle  
**Consumer Deck:** Lead with emotional benefits, social proof, testimonials
